Having a reliable source of fuel on Mars is essential to the development of a self-sustainable Martian base. Through the Sabatier Fuel Plant Project, the team proposes plans for a full-scale propellant production plant, based on SpaceXâ€™s Mars mission architecture.

Phase 1

the setup

The team builded a reactor setup to study parameters of the Sabatier Reaction. Through this process, they were able to convert carbon dioxide and hydrogen gas to form methane in Mars. As a result of this, the team was able to combine the methane and oxygen to produce the propellant to send rockets back to Earth.
